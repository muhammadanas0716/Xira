What is Apple's product strategy and innovation approach?

SOURCES ANALYZED

Annual report and financial statements Annual report and financial statements Annual report and financial statements

Apple's product strategy centers on creating integrated hardware, software, and services ecosystems that deliver seamless user experiences. The company focuses on innovation through continuous product development, with a strong emphasis on design, quality, and user privacy. Apple's approach includes expanding into new product categories, enhancing existing product lines, and building a robust services business that complements its hardware offerings 1 2.
